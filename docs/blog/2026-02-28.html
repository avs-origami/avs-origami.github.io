Origami: Whimsical Edition                                            02/28/2026
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Yep, I've been doing more origami. And this time around, I've decided to take a
little break from my usual inclination to fold complex, realistic creations to
fold something a little more whimsical.


Sasade Shinji's Crane Humanoid. Instructions in ?this book.
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I still have to shape the arms and legs, though. It's supposed to look buff. But
then again, I'm a bit scared of making it look like I just squished the paper,
so I might just leave it like this for now.

My friend also folded one with me, which is always fun. She thinks it looks a
bit like an anime mech robot (which it kind of does).
